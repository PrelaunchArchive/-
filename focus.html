Our Preservation Focus

Our initiative is dedicated to preserving the legacy of PlayStation by meticulously documenting and restoring prototypes across all platforms, genres, and artifact types, ensuring their historical significance is maintained for future generations.

Prototype Types

  • Early Builds & Unreleased Games: Archiving alpha, beta, and unreleased titles to preserve their developmental history.
  • Debug Hardware & Development Kits: Documenting and restoring test units, devkits, and engineering samples for research purposes.
  • Concept Demos & Tech Showcases: Preserving early tech demos and promotional builds that showcase PlayStation’s innovation.
  • Pre-release Discs & Cartridges: Securing and imaging pre-release media to maintain bit-perfect records.

Supported Platforms

We cover all PlayStation platforms, from PlayStation 1 to PlayStation 5, including handhelds such as PSP and PS Vita, ensuring comprehensive preservation across the entire PlayStation ecosystem.
