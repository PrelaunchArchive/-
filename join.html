Join Our Mission

Contribute to the preservation of PlayStation's rich history by helping us recover, restore, and archive rare prototypes across all generations. Your involvement can make a lasting impact on gaming heritage.

Ways to Contribute

  • Donate Prototype Builds or Hardware: Share access to rare discs, cartridges, or development kits for imaging and preservation.
  • Assist with Dumping, Restoring, and Testing: Help with technical tasks like media imaging, data restoration, or prototype verification.
  • Support Web Archiving and Cataloguing: Contribute to documenting and organizing our preservation efforts online.
  • Spread Awareness in the Gaming Community: Promote our mission through social media, forums, or events to engage more supporters.
