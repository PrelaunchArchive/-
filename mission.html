Our Mission

We are dedicated to preserving the legacy of PlayStation by recovering, restoring, and documenting every prototype across all generations, ensuring that gaming history is safeguarded for future generations.

Our Commitment

Our mission is to meticulously locate, preserve, and restore PlayStation prototypes, including early builds, debug hardware, and pre-release media, while adhering to ethical and legal standards.
