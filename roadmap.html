Future Plans

Explore our roadmap outlining key goals and upcoming milestones in our mission to preserve and document PlayStation prototypes across all generations and genres.

Phase 1: Catalog Expansion

Expand our prototype catalog by sourcing and verifying new submissions, focusing on rare PS1 and PS2 builds.

Phase 2: Restoration Tools

Develop and refine tools for non-destructive restoration of corrupted media and assets.

Phase 3: Community Archive

Launch a public, research-friendly archive with detailed documentation and metadata for all preserved prototypes.
